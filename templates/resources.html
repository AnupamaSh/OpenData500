The GovLab Academy is an online training community that uses technology and innovation to tackle public problems. A partnership between The GovLab and MIT Media Lab’s Online Learning Initiative, the site offers videos, podcasts, readings and activities designed to enable the purpose-driven learner to deepen his or her practical knowledge.

The Governance Lab (The GovLab) at New York University strives to improve people’s lives by changing how we govern. Founded in 2012, with funding from the MacArthur and Knight Foundations, the GovLab designs technology, policy and strategies for fostering more open and collaborative approaches to governance, and tests what works.
