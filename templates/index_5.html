{% extends "main.html" %} {% autoescape None %} {% block body %} Open Data 500 Roundtables: Department of Commerce, June 18, 2014 The first Open Data 500 Roundtable was held in Washington, DC on June 18, 2014. It was co-hosted by the GovLab and the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) together with the U.S. Department of Commerce. Under Secretary of Commerce for Economic Affairs Mark Doms led the Department's planning and engagement with the event, and participated for the entire day. About 20 other officials and staff from the Department of Commerce participated as well, with appearances by Secretary Penny Pritzker and Acting Deputy Secretary Bruce Andrews. Nick Sinai, U.S. Deputy Chief Technology Officer, and Erie Meyer, Senior Advisor to the U.S. CTO, represented OSTP at the event. You can read blog posts about the event from Secretary of Commerce Penny Prtizker here (LINK) and from GovLab Senior Advisor Joel Gurin here (LINK). A full list of attendees and some photos of the event are below.
